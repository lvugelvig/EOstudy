It is my pleasure to announce the third Comparative Embryology of
Marine Invertebrates (CEMI) Course that will take at CEBIMAR (Centro de

Brazil from Nov 27th - Dec 11th, 2016. It is a two-week intensive
course exploring some exciting local species. Registration is 16 USD
(free for USP students) and covers accomodation costs (food and board)
during the course, as well as bus transportation from São Paulo to São
Sebastião before and after the course. Please, forward the link below to
any graduate students interested. Application deadline is September 12th!
