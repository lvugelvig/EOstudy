We are pleased to announce that the next Simultaneously Hermaphroditic 
Organisms Workshop (SHOW) will be held in Lund, Sweden, the 9th and 10th 
of March 2017.

SHOW is an annual meeting of researchers working on questions related to 
sex in hermaphrodites. If you would like to be added to the SHOW mailing 
list, please contact jessica.abbott@biol.lu.se.

More details about the meeting, including registration, will be provided 
later. For now, just mark the dates in your calendar!

Looking forward to seeing you in Lund!
