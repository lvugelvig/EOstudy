THREE WEEKS TO SEPEEG!  REGISTER NOW!

The43rd annual Southeastern Population Ecology and Evolutionary Genetics
meeting (SEPEEG, pronounced "seepage") will be held on the weekend of
October 21-23 at Camp Cherry Lake in Madison, Florida.

SEPEEG provides a friendly, informal setting for students, postdocs,
and faculty to meet, talk science and relax around the campfire.

The meeting will be hosted by the University of Florida Department
of Biology.  Registration is now open and the cost is $125/person (link

here: http://mcdaniellab.biology.ufl.edu/sepeeg-2016/).  Funds are
available for student travel, generously provided by the American Society
of Naturalists.  Please contact the organizers, Stuart McDaniel

(stuartmcdaniel@ufl.edu) or Charlie Baer (cbaer@ufl.edu) for further
information.

See you at seepage!
