"Insect Infection and Immunity in Insects: from Mechanisms to Evolution"

Emmanuel College, Cambridge, UK

Friday, 16th December, 2016.

This is a one-day meeting that aims to bring together people working on
different facets of insect infection and immunity. As guest speaker we will
have Dr. Sylvia Cremer who has pioneered the study of cooperative defences
in eusocial insects.

Registration and more information at:
http://infectionandimmunity.strikingly.com/

The Royal Entomological Society sponsors this meeting.
