BioSyst.EU represents European scientists dealing with topics of
systematic biology.

The third meeting of the BioSyst.EU will be organised by the Swedish
Systematics Association in corporation with the University of Gothenburg
and will be held August 15-18, 2017 at the Wallenberg Conference Centre,
Gothenburg, Sweden.

The meeting will feature half-day symposia held by member societies
of BioSyst.EU and external organisations as well as open sessions with
mixed presentations.

More information on the meeting, and registration forms, are available
at the following sites:
