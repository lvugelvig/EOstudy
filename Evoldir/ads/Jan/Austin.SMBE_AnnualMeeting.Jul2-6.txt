The Annual Meeting of the Society for Molecular Biology and Evolution
will be in downtown Austin Texas July 2-6 2017.

Abstract submissions are due FEB 1.

The scientific program spans a wide variety of cutting edge topics in
evolutionary biology.  SMBE offers many travel awards and other prizes
for students and postdocs, as well as assistance with care of children
or other dependents.  Please go to http://www.smbe2017.org/ to submit
an abstract and to see the list of plenary speakers and symposium topics.

We hope to see you in Austin!
