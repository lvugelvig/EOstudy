Symposium on Non-coding RNAs: Function and Evolution

Villars-sur-Ollon, Switzerland

September 12-14, 2016

The aim of the 2016 Villars Symposium on "Non-coding RNAs: function and
evolution" is to provide an interdisciplinary look at the roles and
evolution of non-coding RNAs in both prokaryotes and eukaryotes. An
outstanding list of speakers will be discussing the latest advancement
in the evolution and roles of various non-coding RNAs, including
microRNAs, piRNAs, long-non-coding RNAs and circular RNAs, in both
prokaryotes, animal and plants.

Registration deadline: June 15, 2016
