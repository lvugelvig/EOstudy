Fall Workshop in Introductory Bioinformatics
University of Maryland College Park, Maryland
September 15-18, 2016

There is no prerequisite for this course other than a willingness to
learn and to work hard throughout the week. All workshop exercises will be
implemented via VirtualBox and focus primarily on Illumina data; however,
we will also include PacBio data during the genome assembly tutorial.

Course participation is limited to 25 students to ensure an intimate
learning environment. Course tuition is $800 and is open to graduate
students, postdocs, staff, faculty, and industry researchers on a first
come basis.
