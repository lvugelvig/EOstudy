Last Call for Applications and Bursaries: Closes FRIDAY 8 April!

Human Genome Analysis: Genetic Analysis of Multifactorial Diseases
20-26 July 2016, Wellcome Genome Campus, Hinxton UK
Application and Bursary Deadline: 8 April

Summary: This intensive, computational course is aimed at scientists
actively involved in genetic analysis of multifactorial traits. The
programme provides a comprehensive overview of the statistical methods
currently used to map disease susceptibility genes in human populations.

Bursaries: Limited bursaries are available (up to 50%
of the course fee) and are awarded on merit.  URL:
https://registration.hinxton.wellcome.ac.uk/events/item.aspx?e=3D566
